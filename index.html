App Information

This app shows the location of fictitious drainage complaints, stormwater management facilities, stormwater outfalls, and stormwater pipes located within Prince William County.

Please click on the searchbutton on the right to search for an address, facility ID, outfall ID, pipe ID, or complaint ID.

    To update the status or address of a complaint:
  1. Click on the Sign-inbutton and use your Penn State ArcGIS Online account or grant permission for the app
  2. Click on the Editbutton.
  3. Click on the edit feature button.
  4. Click on any drainage compliant feature.
  5. Select the status from the dropdown list or change the address.
  6. Click on the update button.
When you finished updating, click on the Sign-outbutton.
